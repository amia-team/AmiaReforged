üìñ Overview of the Crafting System

Welcome to the Amia Crafting System!

The crafting system allows players to enhance their equipment using magical mythal crystals. Each mythal tier unlocks different properties that can be added to weapons, armor, and accessories. Every item has a maximum point budget, and each property consumes a certain number of points when applied.


Key Concepts:

  • Power Cost: The number of points a property consumes from an item's budget
  • Item Budget: The maximum number of points an item can have
  • Mythal Tiers: Different quality levels of mythals unlock different properties
  • Free Properties: Some properties cost 0 points but may still require specific mythal tiers

üíé Information about Mythals

Mythal crystals are splinters of solidified magic, rumored to have broken off from the Weave during a conflict in Amia‚Äôs distant past. Intuitive minds soon discovered that these fragments were a valuable energy source and crafting material. Years later, the knowledge mythalcraft has spread throughout the nearby Realms, though mystery still shrouds the origin and ultimate fate of the shards. The Church of Mystra, for its part, seems content in this.


The size of a mythal directly correlates to its magical potential and rarity. Certain stations throughout the gameworld will allow characters to combine or split crystals apart as desired.


Mythal Tiers:

  • Minor - Basic enhancements
  • Lesser - Improved properties
  • Intermediate - Advanced abilities
  • Greater - Powerful enhancements
  • Flawless - Superior properties
  • Perfect - Exceptional abilities
  • Divine - Divine power

üéØ Quick Reference Summary

  • FREE Properties (0 cost): All Visual Effects, Roleplay Skills (Appraise, Bluff, Lore, etc.), Many Cast Spell properties
  • Best Value: Attack/Enhancement Bonuses at lower tiers cost only 1 point each
  • Most Expensive: Spell Resistance (32 costs 4 points), Universal Saves (+4 costs 8 points)
  • Weapon Specialists: Two-handed weapons get 8 points vs 6 for one-handed
  • Caster Items: Perfect tier bonus spell slots cost only 1 point per level - excellent for spellcasters!
  • Defensive Builds: Mix Elemental Resistances (1-3 points) with AC Bonuses and Saves for balanced protection
  • On-Hit Builds: Divine on-hit effects (2-3 points each) can help control combat encounters
  • Roleplay Items: Use FREE skills and visual effects to create flavor on items without using power points!
